# Simple Reversi

A simple C++ program that realize the game [Reversi (Othello)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Reversi) in a terminal.

```
  a b c d e f g h
1 . . . . . . . . 1
2 . . . . . . . . 2
3 . . . . . . . . 3
4 . . . o x . . . 4
5 . . . x o . . . 5
6 . . . . . . . . 6
7 . . . . . . . . 7
8 . . . . . . . . 8
  a b c d e f g h
```
